What is ATOM?

Matter is made up of small particles called ATOMS.ATOM is smallest building block of matter.

Democritus and Leucippus suggested that if we go on dividing matter a stage will come when particles obtained cannot be divided further,these particles known as Atom.

WHAT ARE THE MODERN DAY SYMBOL OF ATOM OF DIFFERENT ELEMENTS

Dalton's was the first scientist to use the symbpl for element in a specific sense.

Brezilius suggested that the symbol of element be made from one or two letters of the name of the element.

Now-a-days, IUPAC(International Union of Pure Applied Chemistry)approves name of element.

  • Many of the symbols are the first one or two letters of the element's name in English
  • The first letter of a symbol is always written as Capital(Uppercase)
  • The second letter as a small letter(Lowercase)
For Example:
  1. Hydrogen H
  2. Aluminium Al and not AL
  3. Cobalt,Co and not CO
Symbol of element are formed from the first letter of the name and a letter appearing in the element. Example are:(i) Chlorine Cl,(ii) Zinc Zn etc.
